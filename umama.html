About Us

Raising a generation rooted in Deen and knowledge

Umama Education Center was founded to give students a warm, structured space to learn the Qur'an, Islamic studies, and Arabic language alongside strong moral character — guided by qualified, caring teachers.

Our Programs

Courses designed for every stage of learning

01

Qur'an with Tajweed

Correct recitation, memorization, and understanding of the Qur'an under close teacher guidance.

02

Islamic Studies

Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah and daily life etiquette taught in a simple, practical way for all ages.

03

Arabic Language

Reading, writing, and conversational Arabic to help students connect directly with the Qur'an.

04

Kids' Foundation Class

A gentle introduction to Qaida, basic duas, and good manners for young beginners.

05

Weekend Family Halaqah

A relaxed weekend circle for parents and children to learn and reflect together.

06

Online Classes

Live one-on-one and group sessions for students who can't attend in person.
